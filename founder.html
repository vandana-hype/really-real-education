Deepak Sharma

Really Real Education is an educational initiative of Jalte Diye Foundation. We are working to connect social education, technology, and community action so that learning can support peace, justice, and sustainable progress.

Our foundation direction

SDG10 - Reduced Inequalities

SDG16 - Peace and Justice

About myself

A short introduction to the person behind the message.

I work at the intersection of software development, research, and teaching. I enjoy turning ideas into usable systems that people can learn from, build with, and use to create meaningful social impact.

My vision

The foundation vision, mission, and objectives that guide this work.

Vision: We envision a world in which the entire human population cooperates as one family for global peace.

Mission: Provide social education globally for a peaceful life and harmonious relations.

Objectives:

  • Provide quality social education so communities can cooperate to resolve global threats to humanity.
  • Conduct interactive expert-led sessions to reduce inequalities and improve participation.
  • Run awareness programs to address growing climate change concerns.
  • Build discussion-based learning platforms around rights, responsibilities, peace, and justice.
