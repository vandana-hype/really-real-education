🏛️ Government Schemes & Subsidies

India — verified 2024
🌳

National Afforestation Programme (NAP)

Funding for degraded forest land. ₹8,000–12,000/ha for planting + protection. Apply through State Forest Departments.

🌐 moef.gov.in →📞 011-24695311
🚜

Sub-Mission on Agroforestry (SMAF)

50% subsidy on planting material + installation for farmers. Covers fruit trees, timber, medicinal plants on agricultural land.

🌐 agricoop.nic.in →📞 011-23382680
💰

CAMPA Fund (Compensatory Afforestation)

₹50,000–80,000/ha for restoration projects. For communities, NGOs and forest departments on divertible forest land.

🌐 campa.gov.in →📞 1800-123-4568
☀️

Hariyali Mission / State Schemes

Free saplings from state forest nurseries. Some states offer ₹1,000–5,000 per farmer for tree plantation on bunds.

🌐 Forest Clearance Portal →
📈

Carbon Credit Monetisation

Register with Indian Carbon Market (ICM). Earn ₹400–600 per tonne CO₂. Managed by Bureau of Energy Efficiency (BEE).

🌐 beeindia.gov.in →✉️ carbon@naccc.org
🔬

Free Soil Testing (Soil Health Card)

Government labs test soil for free / nominal fee. Get pH, NPK, micro-nutrients. Apply at local Krishi Vigyan Kendra.

🌐 Soil Health Portal →📞 Kisan Call: 1551
