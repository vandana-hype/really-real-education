      Government Schemes & Subsidies
      India, updated 2025
      ▾

      National Afforestation Programme (NAP)

      ₹8,000–12,000/ha for planting + 3 years maintenance.

      Sub-Mission on Agroforestry (SMAF)

      50% subsidy on planting material, drip irrigation.

      CAMPA Fund

      ₹50,000–80,000/ha for restoration of degraded forest land.

      PM-KUSUM (Solar for Agri-Forestry)

      60% subsidy for solar irrigation pumps.

      Soil Health Card

      Free soil testing at Krishi Vigyan Kendra.

      Indian Carbon Market

      Earn ₹400–600 per tonne CO₂.

      Green India Mission (GIM)

      ₹25,000/ha for ecosystem restoration.

      Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana

      Support for fruit tree plantation, silvopasture.

      Atal Bhujal Yojana

      Subsidies for water harvesting near plantations.

      Nagar Van Yojana

      ₹10 lakh per city forest for Miyawaki forests.

      National Biodiversity Mission

      Funding for native species restoration.

      Forest Fire Prevention Scheme

      ₹5,000/ha for fire line creation.
