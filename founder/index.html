About the founder

Exploring the great depths of science and life.

Hi, I am Deepak. I build and share software, educational resources, and practical learning material. My work spans development, research, and teaching, with a consistent goal: making knowledge clearer and more accessible.

Tech stack across projects includes HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, React, R, Matlab, Java, C, and C++. The direction is simple: useful tools, thoughtful design, and learning that helps real communities.
