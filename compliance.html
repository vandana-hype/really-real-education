Compliance and ethics statement

Really Real Education is an educational initiative of Jalte Diye Foundation. We aim for transparency, privacy, and responsible content publishing.

Last updated: Feb 24, 2026

  • No advertising or tracking scripts are used on this site.
  • We do not collect personal data, cookies, or analytics on the education domain.
  • External links (including Amazon and the foundation site) open in a new tab and follow their own policies.
  • Content is published to educate and inform, not to mislead or promote harmful material.
  • We respect intellectual property and cite sources where required.
  • Contact the foundation if any content needs correction or removal.
